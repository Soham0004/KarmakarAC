Frequently Asked Questions

Got doubts? We believe in 100% transparency.

What is your minimum visiting/inspection charge?

Our standard inspection fee is just ₹299 anywhere in Kolkata. If you proceed with the repair or service with us after the inspection, this fee is adjusted/waived off in the final bill!

Do you provide a warranty on repairs and parts?

Yes! We provide a 30-day service warranty on all our repair work. Any genuine spare parts we replace come with their respective manufacturer's warranty (usually up to 1 year).

How quickly can you reach my location?

For emergency breakdowns during summer, we strive to reach your doorstep within 2 to 4 hours. For standard servicing, we offer same-day or next-day appointments based on your convenience.

Do you service all AC brands?

Absolutely! We service all major brands including Voltas, Daikin, LG, Samsung, Hitachi, Blue Star, O General, Carrier, Lloyd, and Panasonic. Our technicians are trained across all models.
